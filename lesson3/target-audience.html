Target Audience

Audience Description

The target audience for the Chamber of Commerce site is small and large local businesses looking to grow their business. It also includes their customers, potential customers, and those seeking information about local business in the Chamber's local area.

Personas

Persona 1

Person 1 is a small business owner located in Kaitaia. They would like to grow their business and connect with other businesses.

Persona 2

Person 2 is a resident of Kaitaia and wants to work with a local business.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Scott owns a tree chipping service and wants to connect with tree trimmers and garden nurseries in his area. He starts by searching online through the Chamber of Commerce.

Scenario 2

Lauren is a succesful local lawyer and wants to help out her local small businesses that might need her services. She approaches the local Chamber of Commerce about getting her information on their site.

Scenario 3

Jonathan is working on a project on his house and needs some help from an electrician. Rather than sift through Google and Facebook, he decides to utlize the website for his Chamber of Commerce.

Scenario 4

Rachel is a teacher and would like to solicit donations from local businesses in her classroom and perhaps even a fieldtrip if it's safe. She begins by contacting her local Chamber of Commerce.

Scenario 5

Hailey wants to start her own business but is unsure of the hurdles she might face and unique challenges for her area. She decides to research her local Chamber of Commerce and see what programs they might have for people starting a business.
